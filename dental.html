Welcome To Dental Department

With more than 1 decade of experience,excellence and quality in serving humanity, we are glad to have team of professional experties to help you solving dental problems. Dental Department are committed to provide International Standard PREVENTIVE DENTAL SERVICES by effective and professional way to meet with patient needs, expectations,high-quality less painful,smooth,quick and, thus, more acceptable intraoral care.

Our services include:

  • Cosmetic Dentistry
  • General Dentistry
  • Paediatric Dentistry
  • Orthodontics
  • Surgery
  1. Cosmetic Dentistry

    2. Cosmetic dentistry is very popular nowadays. From whitening and shaping closing spaces and replacing teeth. We are here offering cosmetic dentistry to our patients with our dentists having a wide array of tools and techniques at their disposal to improve the smiles of our customers. Before you decide to undergo any cosmetic procedure it’s very important that you’re aware of the benefits and risks of that procedure. You also have to be very clear on what you expect from the procedure and let our dentists know so that they serve you accordingly and clear out any misconceptions that you may have.

  2. General Dentistry

    3. General dentistry is all about preventing, diagnosing and treating diseases of the oral cavity as well as maintains function and appearance of the teeth. Many oral diseases and abnormalities also indicate other diseases

  3. Paediatric Dentistry

    4. Pediatric dentistry is basically the practice, teaching and research into comprehensive and therapeutic oral health care for children from birth to adolescence. It includes care for children who demonstrate intellectual, physical, mental and emotional problems.

  4. Othodontics

    5. This is a branch of dentistry that treats with a condition of incorrect positioning when the mouth is closed.

  5. Surgery

    6. This a part of many dental procedures. Dental surgery involves artificially modifying dentition, in other words, surgery of the teeth and jaw bones. Simple Extraction Surgical Extraction Implants Broken down roots
